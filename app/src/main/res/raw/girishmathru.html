Girish Mathrubootham


Girish Mathrubootham (born March 29, 1975) is an Indian entrepreneur and angel investor. He is the founder and CEO ofFreshdesk Inc., a cloud-based customer engagement software company.Girish studied engineering at SASTRA University, and did his MBA from Madras University.

Before starting Freshdesk, he worked at Zoho Corporation for close to a decade, and was the Vice President of Product Management of their ManageEngine division.He founded Freshdesk in 2010 following a nightmarish customer experience from his personal life that involved shipping his TV from the USA to India. In October 2010, along with his then Technical Architect, Shan Krishnasamy, he quit Zoho and founded Freshdesk. Originally working out of a conference room inside a friend's office, then a 700-sq-ft warehouse in the outskirts of Chennai with their own funds.

Girish picked Rajnikanth as his company's unofficial mascot and he can be seen throughout the Freshdesk's office walls

Family

Girish is married and lives with his wife and two children in Chennai, India
